Your security

We never touch your account.

The most common concern before signing up. Here is the honest complete answer before you even have to ask.

🔒
We never see your password
We use OAuth — the same permission system Facebook and Google use. Instagram generates a posting key just for your team. Your password never leaves your device. Not ever.
✓
You approve every post first
Nothing goes live without your approval. You see the exact image, exact caption, exact hashtags before anything posts. Change anything in plain English. Always your call.
⚡
Remove access in 30 seconds
Go to Instagram Settings, Connected Apps, remove Thryvo. Access stops instantly. No phone calls, no waiting, no complicated process. Your account is always yours.
🛡️
Your data belongs to you
Your brand data and content is stored in your own isolated private instance. No other client can see it. Never shared, never sold. It belongs to you always.
📋
Posting permission only
The permission we use can only publish approved scheduled content. Cannot read DMs, change settings, go live, or delete posts. Strictly posting. Nothing more.
👤
Built by a real maker
AJ built this for his own silver business first. Tested two months before charging anyone. A real product maker who lived this problem — not a faceless tech company.
How does the connection to my social accounts actually work?
Your team sends you a link after onboarding. You tap it and log into each platform once — a posting permission is created, not your password stored. 3 minutes. Never again.
Can I still post on my own accounts myself?
Always. Your account is fully yours. Your team posts consistently in the background — you can post manually any time alongside it. Nothing changes about how you use your account.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your content and brand data is yours. We delete it from our system within 30 days of cancellation on request. You own everything your team created.
Does anyone at Thryvo ever log into my account?
Never. We use Buffer — trusted by 140,000+ businesses. Nobody at Thryvo logs into your account. We only publish approved content through the scheduling system. Your login is yours alone.
What if I don't like the content you create?
Nothing posts without your approval. Tell VEXA what to change in plain English — adjusts instantly and files your preference permanently. Your team gets better every week.
Which platforms can you post to?
Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Pinterest, YouTube Shorts, and X — depending on your plan. Starter covers Instagram and Facebook. Growth adds TikTok. Premium covers 6 platforms plus YouTube Shorts.

For a long time I knew social media was the key to growing my brand. So I did what everyone says to do. I learned Canva. Watched the YouTube tutorials. Then Buffer. More tutorials. Then ChatGPT for captions. Then a video app. Then a scheduling tool. Five apps. Days of learning. Then hours every day actually using them — staging photos, editing, writing captions, scheduling posts. All after already working a full day at the bench.

I was spending $500 to $1,000 a month on ads on top of all of it. And I genuinely did not know if it was working. Was it the ads? The Instagram post from Tuesday? To find out I had to log into the ads dashboard. Then Instagram insights. Then Etsy stats. Then try to connect the dots across three platforms that do not talk to each other.

Everyone said the same thing. Keep posting. Keep paying. It will work eventually.

Meanwhile I had statues to design. Materials to order. A bench to get back to. Six kids who wanted their dad. I would work all day making things. Work all night on social media. Miss dinner. Miss bedtime. Wake up and do it again. And still feel like it was not enough.

The question was never whether I should be on social media. The question was — why does doing it right have to cost this much? This many apps. This much money. This much time. This much not knowing.

So I stopped trying to make the broken system work. And built a new one.

One message to VEXA. Photo and a few words. Everything else handled. Monday morning I see exactly what drove sales — which platform, which post, which format. One report. No dashboards. No guessing.

No more paying for ads hoping they work. No more five apps to learn. No more choosing between my craft and my brand.

Send a photo. Say a few words. Go design something new.

That is why Thryvo exists.
